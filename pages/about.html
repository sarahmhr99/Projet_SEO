About the person behind the website


Who?
The person behind the website is a 20 year old girl from London. You can contact her here!
What?
This is a website supporting mental health awareness and giving out information others might want to know about how people deal with suffering from mostly misunderstood mental illnesses.
When?
The website was made during the 2nd semester of the student behind the website's first year of Undergraduate Technological Degree.
Where?
At the University of Paris Est Marne La Vallee in Meaux, France.

You may go there by looking up the following address :

17 Rue Jablinot, 77100 MEAUX

You may contact them by calling the following number :

+33 1 64 36 44 98

Why?
Due to the fact that she and her friends know multiple people suffering of mental illnesses that people often aren't well informed about, she decided to take the opportunity of creating this website for her web integration project.
Please remember to reach out to your relatives if you ever need any kind of help.

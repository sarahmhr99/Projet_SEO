What is a mental illness?


In this article, we will see what a mental illness is and how you may "diagnose" one in order to seek help as soon as possible.

Mental health is defined by emotional as well as conscious and unconscious well-being.

A mental illness is a condition that isn't necessarly visible to others. It affects a person's way of thinking, their emotions and thus their behaviour.

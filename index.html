Welcome to a different universe!


For mental illnesses that are often misunderstood, because the human brain is a universe that is yet to be fully discovered.


Here, you will get to learn about multiple mental disorders that are often misunderstood, such as depression, schizophrenia, autism and more. You will also learn, throughout this blog, the possible consequences of those disorders as well as what you may do to help.

This website has informative purposes only, but please do keep in mind that the sources come from people who suffer from the described illness in its respective article and that no medical professional was contacted. The information is therefore the personnal opinion of an anonymous being suffering from the described disorder, and symptoms might differ from one person or another suffering from the same illness.
